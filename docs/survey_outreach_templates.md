# Survey Outreach Templates (INR 200 Policy)

Use these templates across call intro, WhatsApp invite, reminder, and completion confirmations.

## Mandatory Compensation Lines

- "You will receive INR 200 after successfully completing the full survey."
- "Compensation delivery method and timing will be shared after completion verification."

Compensation delivery method and timing will be shared after completion verification.  
Reply YES to opt in.
